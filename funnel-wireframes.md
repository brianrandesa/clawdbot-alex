## MOBILE OPTIMIZATION NOTES

### Critical Mobile UX Elements:
- **Thumb-friendly buttons:** Minimum 44px height
- **Single-column layout** for quiz questions
- **Progress indicator** always visible  
- **Swipeable testimonials** on mobile
- **Collapsible FAQ sections** to save space
- **Click-to-call buttons** prominently displayed
- **Fast loading:** Under 3 seconds on 3G
- **Form optimization:** Minimal typing, maximum selections

### Mobile-Specific Copy Adjustments:
- **Shorter headlines** for small screens
- **Bullet points** instead of long paragraphs
- **Scannable sections** with clear visual breaks
- **Sticky CTA buttons** on long pages
