# ESA Client Success Journey Framework

## Pricing Structure
- **3 months:** $15K
- **6 months:** $25K (PREFERRED - better ROI for client)
- **Done-for-you sales:** 50% commission

## Core Deliverables (All Done-For-You)
1. **Ads** - Targeted lead generation
2. **Funnel** - Conversion optimization  
3. **CRM** - Lead management system
4. **Automations** - Follow-up sequences
5. **Copy** - All messaging and content
6. **SMS/Email** - Multi-channel nurturing
7. **Sales Reps** - Either training or DFY sales team

## 6-Month Journey Map

### Month 1: Foundation & Data Gathering
- **Week 1-2:** Setup and launch
- **Week 3-4:** Initial data collection
- **Key Metric:** Leads flowing to client's phone
- **Client Expectation:** "Getting data, dialing in positioning"

### Month 2-3: Optimization Phase  
- **Funnel optimization** based on data
- **Ad targeting refinement**
- **Sales process improvements**
- **Key Metric:** Conversion rate improvements

### Month 4-6: Scaling & Advanced Positioning
- **Scale ad spend** based on proven metrics
- **Advanced automation implementation**
- **Market positioning refinement**
- **Key Metric:** Revenue growth and ROI

## Value Proposition for 6-Month Commitment
"The first 3 months of any business running ads for the first time is about getting data and understanding positioning. Those things take time. While I offer 3-month packages, I secure 6 months with people because that's when the real scaling happens."

## Done-For-You vs Done-With-You Decision Tree
- **Done-For-You:** No existing sales team, want hands-off approach
- **Done-With-You:** Have sales team, want to build internal capability
- **Both options:** Full marketing stack included regardless
