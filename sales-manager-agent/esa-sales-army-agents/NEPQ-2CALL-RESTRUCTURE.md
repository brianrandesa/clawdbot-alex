## ðŸ“ž CALL 1: AUDIT/DIAGNOSTIC (25-30 Minutes)

### **STAGE 1: CONNECTION (5 Minutes)**
#### **Expert Positioning Frame:**
*"Hi [Name], thanks for taking time today. I want to be upfront - this isn't a sales call. This is a diagnostic session. I'm going to audit your [EVENT NAME] to identify exactly where the biggest opportunities are for growth. I use the same system we've used to optimize over 200 events. At the end, you'll have a complete scorecard showing your strengths, gaps, and revenue potential. Then we can determine if we're even the right fit for each other. Sound good?"*

#### **Rapport Building:**
- Acknowledge their expertise/industry
- Reference their specific event/challenge  
- Establish consultant-expert dynamic

### **STAGE 2: ENGAGEMENT - EVENT AUDIT (18-20 Minutes)**

#### **Foundation Questions (Event Type & Experience):**
*"Let's start with the basics. Tell me about this event - is this a business conference, workshop, trade show, or what type of event are we dealing with?"*

*"How many times have you hosted events like this?"*

*"What was your attendance at your last similar event?"*

*"And what's your attendance goal for this upcoming event?"*

#### **Monetization Questions:**
*"What are you charging for tickets to this event?"*

*"This is important - do you sell anything AT the event itself? Coaching programs, products, services, anything like that?"*

#### **Marketing & Sales Questions:**
*"How much are you planning to spend on marketing between now and the event?"*

*"Who's currently running your ads - professional agency, in-house team, freelancer, or no one?"*

*"How many leads do you currently have for this specific event?"*

*"How many tickets have you already sold?"*

*"Do you have sales reps actively calling these leads, or is it all self-serve registration?"*

#### **Infrastructure Questions:**
*"Do you use a CRM system to manage and follow up with your leads?"*

*"How would you describe your website - professionally designed and up-to-date, functional but basic, or pretty minimal?"*

*"What's your Instagram following size?"*

*"How many people are on your email list?"*

#### **Authority & Priority Questions:**
*"Are you the person who makes the final decision on marketing investments like this?"*

*"What's your biggest bottleneck right now - marketing and sales, event management, content and speakers, or logistics and operations?"*

#### **CONSEQUENCE/PAIN QUESTIONS (Natural NEPQ Integration):**
*"What happens if you don't hit your attendance goal for this event?"*

*"How much revenue are you leaving on the table if you don't have a backend offer?"*

*"What's the cost of having all these leads but no one calling them?"*

*"If your marketing spend doesn't deliver results, what's that going to mean for future events?"*

### **TRANSITION TO CALL 2 (2 Minutes):**
#### **Score Announcement:**
*"Based on what you've told me, you scored [X] out of 75 points on our Event Success Framework. This puts you in the [TIER] category."*

#### **Problem Amplification:**
*"Here's what I see: You have [STRENGTH], but there are some significant gaps in [GAP 1] and [GAP 2]. Based on your numbers, you're potentially leaving $[AMOUNT] on the table with every event."*

#### **Custom Solution Setup:**
*"Rather than give you a generic solution, I'd like to build something custom for your specific situation. The gaps I identified have very specific solutions, and I want to show you exactly how we'd address them for [EVENT NAME]."*

#### **Call 2 Scheduling:**
*"Can we get the decision makers together for 30 minutes next week? I'll put together a complete optimization strategy based on your audit results, and we can see if it makes sense to work together."*
