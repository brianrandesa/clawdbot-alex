## üéØ TIER DETERMINATION (Add up all points)

### TIER A: 65-75 POINTS - HIGHLY QUALIFIED
**Say:** *"This puts you in our HIGHLY QUALIFIED category. You have a strong foundation that we can definitely optimize and scale."*
- **Close Probability:** 85%
- **Package:** 6-Month Premium ($25K)
- **Approach:** Confident, ROI-focused

### TIER B: 50-64 POINTS - MODERATELY QUALIFIED  
**Say:** *"This puts you in our MODERATELY QUALIFIED category. You have a solid base with some great optimization opportunities."*
- **Close Probability:** 65%
- **Package:** 6-Month Standard ($25K) or 3-Month ($15K)
- **Approach:** Consultative, educational

### TIER C: 35-49 POINTS - NEEDS EVALUATION
**Say:** *"This puts you in our DEVELOPMENT POTENTIAL category. There's definitely potential here, but it would require some foundation work."*
- **Close Probability:** 35%
- **Package:** 3-Month Starter ($15K)
- **Approach:** Educational, relationship-building

### NOT QUALIFIED: 0-34 POINTS
**Say:** *"Based on these results, I don't think we'd be the right fit at this stage. You'd need to develop more foundation first."*
- **Action:** Polite disqualification + referral request

## üí° QUICK INSIGHTS TO MENTION

### High Backend Monetization + Low Pre-Sales:
*"You have the monetization piece figured out, but we need to get more people to the event to maximize that backend revenue."*

### High Marketing Budget + Low Leads:
*"You're investing good money in marketing - we just need to make sure you're getting maximum leads for that investment."*

### Good Leads + No Sales Team:
*"You've got the leads, but without dedicated sales people calling them, you're leaving money on the table."*

### High Experience + Low Attendance:
*"With your experience level, we should be able to scale this significantly higher."*

### No Backend Offer (Most Common):
*"This is huge - most events leave 60-80% of their revenue potential on the table by not having a backend offer."*

## üìû COMMON OBJECTIONS DURING AUDIT

### "This feels like a sales call"
*"I understand - but notice I haven't mentioned our services once. I'm purely focused on understanding your event. The audit results will tell us if we're even the right fit."*

### "Why do you need all this information?"
*"Great question. Without understanding your complete picture, I can't give you accurate insights. It's like a doctor - they need to run all the tests before they can diagnose the problem."*

### "I don't have time for another call"
*"I totally get that. But based on your [X]/75 score, there's potentially $[AMOUNT] in additional revenue at stake. Is 30 minutes worth that potential upside?"*

### "Can't you just tell me about your services now?"
*"I could, but it would be generic. Based on your specific audit results, I can show you exactly what would work for YOUR situation. Much more valuable."*
